A Little About Me

  • Dog mom too. And... cat. Yes, we rescued a cate from my office building. Pegasus.

  • Art published in one of Ken Wilber's books. Created a video called "The Twitter Experiment" to document a project we did while I was looking for creative ways to use Twitter, now X, in the classroom. Check it out below:
  • Rode with the USAF team across Iowa in RAGBRAI (an unforgettable adventure!).

  • Neurodivergent with multi-modal synesthesia, making my perspective on the world uniquely vibrant.
