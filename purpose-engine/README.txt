# Purpose Engine – AI for Classroom Purpose & Motivation
Uplift Education submission for AI Challenge – Innovation for Educators

Connects schoolwork to students' future selves/goals via AI reframing, Future Self coaching, and early signals.

## Demos (open index.html)
- Educator Dashboard: Real-time alerts & purpose-framed check-ins
- Student Dashboard: Purpose points & journey visualization
- Assignment Transform: Before/after reframing without changing standards
- AI Techniques: Joint embedding, semantic alignment, edge inference

Built to boost engagement in Dallas/TX classrooms while keeping teachers in control.
