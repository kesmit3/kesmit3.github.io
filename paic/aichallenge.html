Why This Work Exists

AI is already part of our children’s world. The question is not whether it will be used, but how intentionally we choose to shape it.

I chose to work on this because understanding matters. Over the past two years, I have studied, experimented, and listened—through formal coursework, industry conferences, applied technical training, and ongoing conversations with students and educators.

My background in theology and ethics informs how I think about responsibility, environmental impact, and human dignity. As a parent, I am motivated by something simpler: I want technology to help children grow into capable, connected adults.
